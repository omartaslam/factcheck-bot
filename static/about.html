About Fred

Fred is an independent, AI-assisted fact-checking service committed to evidence-based verification free from geographic, political, religious, or cultural bias. This page discloses our ownership, funding, editorial structure, and independence commitments in accordance with international fact-checking standards.

What Fred Is

Fred is a fact-checking tool available via WhatsApp and the web at fredcheck.com. Users submit claims — text, URLs, images, or social media posts — and Fred returns a sourced verdict drawn from a network of 65+ outlets across six world regions. Verdicts are produced by a large language model operating under a published constitutional charter, with human editorial oversight.

Fred is not affiliated with any news organisation, political party, government body, or advocacy group.

Funding Model

Fred is funded entirely through user subscription and pay-per-check revenue. We do not accept grants, donations, advertising revenue, or funding from political organisations, governments, advocacy groups, or any entity with a declared interest in the outcome of fact-checks.

No funder, advertiser, or commercial partner has any ability to influence, review, suppress, or direct individual fact-checks or verdicts prior to publication. Editorial decisions are made independently.

Independence statement: Fred Check has no institutional, financial, or editorial relationship with any political party, government, military, intelligence service, advocacy organisation, or media conglomerate. This independence is a condition of our operation, not a preference.

Editorial Oversight

Fred's AI system operates under a published constitutional charter — the Fred Charter — which governs how every verdict is assessed. The charter is applied algorithmically and reviewed by human editors. All verdicts are subject to correction upon challenge (see our Corrections Policy).

Human editorial staff are responsible for: maintaining and updating the charter, reviewing contested verdicts, applying corrections, and overseeing the source network. Staff details are available on request.

Source Network

Fred draws on a curated network of 65+ outlets covering Western, Arabic, Francophone, Spanish-language, Russian, Chinese, and South Asian media. Sources are weighted by reliability, not by geographic or political origin. The full source list and tiering methodology are published in our Methodology.

AI Disclosure

Fred's verdicts are produced by an AI language model (Claude, by Anthropic) operating under strict written rules. The AI does not determine its own editorial standards — those are set by the human editorial team through the charter. We disclose this because transparency about AI involvement is, in our view, a basic obligation for any fact-checking service.

We do not represent AI-generated verdicts as human journalism. Fred is a tool. Human editorial judgement governs the rules under which it operates, and human editors handle all corrections and disputes.
